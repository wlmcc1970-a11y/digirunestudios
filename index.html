How It Works

From Handshake to Launch

The process is simple by design. Publishers bring the IP and the community. DigiRune handles everything else.

I
Demo & Discussion

We share the working Twisted app live, so you see exactly what your community would receive. If it's the right fit, we move forward.

II
Partnership Agreement

A straightforward revenue-sharing agreement. You keep full IP ownership; DigiRune gets an exclusive license to publish under your brand. 70/30 split in our favor — you earn passively on every download.

III
Development & Approval

We build the app to your game's full depth — characters, rules, factions, mechanics, lore. You review and approve before anything goes to market. Typical timeline: 5–6 weeks from content access to submission.

IV
Launch & Ongoing Revenue

The app launches on Google Play and the Apple App Store under your branding. DigiRune handles all maintenance, updates, and support. You announce it to your community and collect your share — indefinitely.

About the Founder

Veteran-Owned. Builder-Operated.

Will McCullough
Founder & Owner

Will McCullough is a Marine Corps veteran, entrepreneur, and lifelong tabletop gamer. He served as a Combat Engineer, Senior Drill Instructor, and Close Combat Instructor-Trainer — roles where precision, accountability, and attention to detail aren’t optional. That standard is baked into every app DigiRune builds.

He went on to found one of South Carolina’s first MMA gyms, serve as Governor-appointed Chairman of the SC State Athletic Commission — where he brought the first-ever UFC event to the state — and build a 25-year real estate career representing over 1,000 clients as broker-owner of an independent franchise office within one of the world’s largest international real estate brands. He currently serves as a National Director for GAMMA USA, the U.S. branch of the global governing body for amateur mixed martial arts.

Will has also served as chapter President of BNI, Vice Chairman of a regional Parks & Recreation Board, and three terms as elected Director of his regional REALTORS Association — leadership roles grounded in business development, community stewardship, and navigating regulatory frameworks.

What that means for publishers: DigiRune is run by someone who has built businesses from scratch, navigated government regulation, managed complex partnerships, and delivered under pressure — consistently, for decades. Your IP is in experienced hands.
