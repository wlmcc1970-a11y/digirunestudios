How It Works

From Handshake to Launch

The process is simple by design. Publishers bring the IP and the community. DigiRune handles everything else.

I
Demo & Discussion

We share the working Twisted app as a live proof-of-concept. You see exactly what your community would receive — no mockups, no promises, just a finished product. If it's the right fit, we move forward.

II
Partnership Agreement

A straightforward revenue-sharing agreement. You retain full IP ownership. DigiRune receives an exclusive license to publish the companion app under your official brand. 70/30 split in DigiRune's favor — you earn passively on every download.

III
Development & Approval

We build the app to your game's full depth — characters, rules, factions, mechanics, lore. You review and approve before anything goes to market. Typical timeline: 2–3 weeks from content access to submission.

IV
Launch & Ongoing Revenue

The app publishes to Google Play and the Apple App Store under your official game branding. DigiRune manages all maintenance, updates, and support. You announce it to your community and collect your share of every download — indefinitely.

About the Founder

Veteran-Owned. Builder-Operated.

Will McCullough
Founder & Owner

Will McCullough’s path to game technology started with a decade of service in the United States Marine Corps. Stationed at MCRD Parris Island, he served as a Senior Drill Instructor, Close Combat Instructor-Trainer, and Combat Engineer — training thousands of recruits in discipline, resilience, and precision under pressure.

That mindset carried into everything after. He earned dual second-degree black belts in Kyokushin Karate and Matzukazi-Ryu Jujitsu, then in 1996 founded one of South Carolina’s first MMA training facilities — McCullough Submission Fighting — endorsed by then-UFC president Art Davie. The school, now Beaufort MMA, remains one of the oldest continuously running MMA gyms in the state.

In 2014, Governor Nikki Haley appointed Will Chairman of the South Carolina State Athletic Commission, where he oversaw regulation and safety for professional and amateur combat sports statewide. During his tenure, he brought the first-ever UFC event to South Carolina. He continues to serve as a National Director for GAMMA USA, the U.S. branch of the global governing body for mixed martial arts.

Parallel to combat sports, Will built a distinguished career in real estate. Over 25+ years in the Lowcountry, he and his wife Deena have represented more than 1,000 clients as owners of CENTURY 21 Coastal Town Realty. He has been recognized as Beaufort’s top real estate agent by U.S. News & World Report.

A passionate tabletop gamer since childhood, Will brings decades of building businesses from scratch, leading teams, navigating regulation, and delivering results under pressure to DigiRune Studios. Every app is built with the same obsessive accuracy and craftsmanship his career has always demanded.
