How It Works

From Handshake to Launch

The process is simple by design. Publishers bring the IP and the community. DigiRune handles everything else.

I
Demo & Discussion

We share the working Twisted app as a live proof-of-concept. You see exactly what your community would receive — no mockups, no promises, just a finished product. If it's the right fit, we move forward.

II
Partnership Agreement

A straightforward revenue-sharing agreement. You retain full IP ownership. DigiRune receives an exclusive license to publish the companion app under your official brand. 70/30 split in DigiRune's favor — you earn passively on every download.

III
Development & Approval

We build the app to your game's full depth — characters, rules, factions, mechanics, lore. You review and approve before anything goes to market. Typical timeline: 2–3 weeks from content access to submission.

IV
Launch & Ongoing Revenue

The app publishes to Google Play and the Apple App Store under your official game branding. DigiRune manages all maintenance, updates, and support. You announce it to your community and collect your share of every download — indefinitely.

About the Founder

Veteran-Owned. Builder-Operated.

Will McCullough
Founder & Owner

Will McCullough is a Marine Corps veteran, entrepreneur, and lifelong tabletop gamer. He served at MCRD Parris Island as a Senior Drill Instructor and Close Combat Instructor-Trainer — roles where precision, accountability, and attention to detail aren’t optional. That standard is baked into every app DigiRune builds.

He went on to found one of South Carolina’s first MMA gyms, serve as Governor-appointed Chairman of the SC State Athletic Commission — where he brought the first-ever UFC event to the state — and build a 25-year real estate career representing over 1,000 clients as owner of CENTURY 21 Coastal Town Realty. He currently serves as a National Director for GAMMA USA, the U.S. branch of the global governing body for mixed martial arts.

Will has also served as President of BNI Business Alliances in Beaufort, Vice Chairman of the Beaufort County Parks & Recreation Board, and three terms as elected Director of the Beaufort/Jasper County Association of REALTORS — leadership roles grounded in business development, community stewardship, and navigating regulatory frameworks.
